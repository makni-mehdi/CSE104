Description

Trobbles are cute egg-shaped digital pets that simulate real pets.
One has to “feed” them regularly, clean up behind them, and generally take care of their well-being.
One starts with only one trobble, at each round, each trobble present in the game is subject to the following actions:
- Their hunger increses by a certain amount.
- Their health decreases proportionally to their hunger.
Once one trobble's health hits 0, he dies. YOU SHOULD NOT MAKE THAT HAPPEN for such a CUTE creature.
During the game, one gets feedback on each trobble's health but not the exact index of health to keep the game more challenging.
Note that there is a trade-off between health and hunger.
Don't let the Trobble go through trouble!
